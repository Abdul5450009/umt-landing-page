About Us

The University of Management and Technology (UMT), established in 1990, is a premier institution of higher education committed to excellence in teaching, learning, research, and community service. Located in Lahore, Pakistan, UMT is recognized for nurturing leaders of tomorrow through its diverse range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs. With a strong emphasis on innovation, academic excellence, and personal growth, UMT empowers students to become skilled professionals and responsible citizens. The university's world-class faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and vibrant campus life offer a dynamic environment for students to thrive academically and socially. Whether it's business, technology, engineering, social sciences, or humanities, UMT continues to lead the way in shaping a brighter, knowledge-driven future.
